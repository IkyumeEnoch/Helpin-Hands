Relief

Counselling

Provide Emotional Security: offer safe, non-judgemental space for children to express thier feelings and emotions. Stabilize emotional well-being help children manage emotions, develop coping strategies, and improve their mental health

Skill Acquisition

Increase Self-Sulfficiency: Skills acquisition enables children to become more self-sufficient and less reliant on others for their daily needs, Improve Employability: Acquiring skills makes children more employable, increasing their chances of securing a job and earning a steady income
